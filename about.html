About Me

Hello!

My name is Jeralyn Javier. I was born in Guam, USA and grew up in BC on the beautiful Vancouver Island. I am on track to completing my BSc in Combined Computer Science and Psychology. I first pursued a Psychology degree, but later found an interest for coding. This website is my first personal project. It has been quite a rollercoaster trying to discover what I want to achieve for my career goals. I have finally decided that I want to pursue a career in UX/UI design. This is would ideal. I would love to apply my growing knowledge of computer science and psychology to projects where I can express myself more artistically. I enjoy human interaction, creativity, and logic, so it really completes everything I love.

Some values that I have are to be kind and positive. I like to appreciate the days that we have and not take any experience for granted. Every experience is a learning opportunity. Family and friends are also very important to me as they are the best support systems to have. I hope to prosper in many aspects in my life as I have been discovering new enjoyments to fulfill my goals.
